Current Project

I'm building a slow-burning, narrative-driven horror game focused on exploration, atmosphere, and survival without combat. You play as someone who finds themselves trapped in a place with a dark history, slowly unraveling the truth behind disturbing experiments and failed subjects.

There’s no title yet, and the project is still early in development, but it’s growing piece by piece.
Keep an eye on Steam and my socials for updates and a future release date.
