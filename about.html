About Snowrift Interactive

Hi, my name is Linus — I’m the person behind Snowrift Interactive. I live in Sweden and have been working in IT for nearly a decade, mainly in support and system administration.

I don’t have any formal education in game development — just a lot of curiosity and a willingness to figure things out along the way. I'm using Unity, C#, and Blender to build games piece by piece, while also planning to purchase a few assets to help speed things up, to focus on what matters most.

The focus is on atmosphere, storytelling, and meaningful experiences — sometimes quiet and slow, sometimes strange or unsettling, but always made with care. Thanks for stopping by.
